English Spelling and Vocabulary
Yolaroo ABC is an excellent way for people of all ages to start learning English. Yolaroo ABC makes learning how to read and spell easy. There are 10 great activities to help teach over 147 different English words. Each activity has a different goal to help the learner. Yolaroo ABC is great for in the classroom or at home. Teach or learn English. There are 10 apps and activities that are well organized to help teach English. Teach spelling. Teach ABC alphabet. Teach English vocabulary. These are the activities included: 'Letter Introduction' introduces letters to the learner. 'Word Introduction' introduces words and and there letters. 'Vocabulary Introduction' introduces the vocabulary. 'Picture it' matches the words to letters. 'Spelling' introduces spelling of words. 'Word Match' is a learning and testing activity to practice picture and word recognition. 'Picture Hide' shows four pictures and then hides one picture, then asks the learner to find the missing picture. 'Word Hide' shows four words and then hides one word and asks the learner to find the missing word. 'Scramble' is a spelling practice and test. Spell the words normally and then scrambled. 'Double Match' is a picture memory game that asks the learner to match the pictures to the words as well.
